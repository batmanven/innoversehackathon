HOME RENTAL AGREEMENT (LEAVE AND LICENCE)

This agreement is made on this 19th day of April, 2026, between Mr. Rajesh Khanna (hereinafter called the 'Licensor') and Mr. Priyansh Narang (hereinafter called the 'Licensee').

1. TERM & COMMENCEMENT
The Licensor hereby grants the Licensee the right to use the residential premises at Flat 402, Sky-High Apartments, Pune. The agreement is for a period of 11 (eleven) months. However, both parties agree to a mandatory LOCK-IN PERIOD of 12 (twelve) months.

2. LICENCE FEE & SECURITY DEPOSIT
The Licensee shall pay a monthly fee of ₹35,000. Additionally, the Licensee has deposited ₹70,000 as an interest-free Security Deposit. 
The Licensor reserves the right to revise the monthly fee at any time during the term by serving a 30-day written notice. No specific cap on this increase is mentioned herein.

3. EARLY TERMINATION
Should the Licensee vacate the premises before the completion of the 12-month lock-in period, the entire Security Deposit shall be forfeited by the Licensor. Furthermore, the Licensee shall be liable to pay an additional month's rent as liquidated damages.

4. MAINTENANCE & REPAIRS
Minor repairs (electrical, plumbing, etc.) costing less than ₹2,500 per instance shall be the sole responsibility of the Licensee. Major structural repairs will be handled by the Licensor.

5. LATE PAYMENTS
Any delay in payment beyond the 5th of each month will attract a penalty of 3% per month on the cumulative outstanding amount.

6. RIGHT OF ENTRY
The Licensor may enter the premises for inspection with a 2-hour verbal notice to the Licensee.
